Preperation Time

  • Total: approximatley 10 mins
  • Prepartion: 5mins
  • Cooking: 5mins

ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, for taste
  • Pepper, for taste
  • 1tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese,diced vegis,cooked meat

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl beat the eggs witha pinch of salt and pepper until they are well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. cook Omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling pour in the eggs.
  4. Fold and serve: As the omelette continues to cook, carefully lift on edge and fold it over.
  5. Enjoy: Serve hot, with additional salt and pepper if needed.
